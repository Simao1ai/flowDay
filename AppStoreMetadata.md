## Description (4000 characters max)

FlowDay is an AI-powered daily planner that adapts to your energy levels and priorities. Unlike traditional to-do apps, FlowDay understands that not all hours are equal—it schedules your tasks intelligently based on when you're most productive.

**Key Features:**

**Energy-Aware AI Scheduling** — Input your tasks and energy level for the day, and FlowDay's AI automatically suggests the optimal order and timing. High-focus work happens during your peak hours; deep breathing and admin tasks fit into natural energy dips. Powered by your choice of OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini APIs (you control the keys).

**Unified Timeline View** — See your calendar events and tasks on a single timeline. No context switching between apps. Drag to reschedule, tap to adjust focus time.

**Natural Language Input** — "Review Q2 proposal Thursday morning" or "30-min yoga after work" — FlowDay parses it instantly. Create tasks, set reminders, and assign focus blocks without typing everything out.

**Habit Tracking** — Build streaks for the habits that matter. Log them directly from your timeline or quick-add interface. Watch your momentum grow.

**Calendar Integration** — FlowDay reads your existing calendar and respects your meetings. It schedules around your commitments, not against them.

**Focus Timer** — Pomodoro-style or custom focus sessions. Tap the timer on any task, silence notifications, and dive in. Optional integration with your focus music playlist.

**Why FlowDay?**

Most planners treat all tasks the same. Todoist counts a 2-minute email and a 2-hour project equally. Notion lets you organize, but doesn't adapt. FlowDay goes further: it learns your patterns, respects your energy, and makes real-time suggestions so you can focus on what matters instead of what feels urgent.

Whether you're managing multiple projects, building new habits, or just tired of wasted hours on the wrong task at the wrong time, FlowDay adapts to you.

**Privacy First**

Your energy data and calendar are yours alone. FlowDay never stores your data on our servers. AI processing uses your own API keys—you choose the provider and see every request.

## App Review Notes

**For Apple Review Team:**

FlowDay is an AI-powered daily planner that uses natural language processing and scheduling optimization. Please note:

1. **AI Features & API Keys:** FlowDay integrates with OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), and Google Gemini APIs. Users must provide their own API keys in app settings. FlowDay never stores these keys on our servers or transmits user data to FlowDay infrastructure.

2. **Data Privacy:** User tasks, calendar events, and energy data remain on-device. When a user opts to use AI planning features, only the task descriptions and energy metadata are sent directly to the chosen third-party AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google) using the user's own credentials. No user data passes through FlowDay's servers.

3. **Calendar Integration:** The app reads the user's device calendar (via EventKit) to avoid scheduling conflicts. Calendar data never leaves the device.

4. **Functionality:** The core planner, timeline, habit tracking, and focus timer work fully offline if the user does not enable AI features.
